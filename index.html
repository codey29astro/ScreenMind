What product/revenue goal depends on this hire?

Quantify the urgency to prioritise this req.
🧠 Section 2 — WHAT: Skills, Signals & Fit
⚠ Red flag: listing everything as must-have = search will fail
⚠ Red flag: "smart, hardworking, passionate" = unmeasurable = wrong hire
As important as what works. Honest answer = better screen.
High-growth SaaS? FAANG? Domain expertise? Be honest.
⚙️ Section 3 — HOW: Process, Compensation & Timeline
Who interviews? What does each stage evaluate?
⚠ Red flag: "competitive" with no number = late-stage drop = NPS killer
If you need someone in 30 days but market notice is 90 days — solve this now.
Who is the final yes? Any gatekeepers or committee votes?
⚠️ Section 4 — RISKS: Deal-Breakers & Honest Concerns

⚠ Red flag: "this should be easy to fill" for niche senior role
